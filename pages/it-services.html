How support credit works

Included hours are valued at our Level 1 rate ($90/hour), creating a support credit pool your team can draw from.

Support Level Rate 40 hrs credit =
Level 1 (basic troubleshooting, user support) $90/hr 40 hours
Level 2 (system admin, configurations) $120/hr 30 hours
Level 3 (complex issues, escalations) $150/hr 24 hours

Higher-level support consumes credit faster. Mix and match as needed.

Note: Support credit does not roll over month to month. Minimum 5 devices per agreement.
