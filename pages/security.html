Data Sovereignty

Australian Data Stays in Australia

For our Australian clients, we guarantee that your primary data storage and processing occur within Australian borders.

  • Primary Region: Sydney (AWS Sydney / Fly.io Sydney)
  • Backups: Stored in encrypted storage buckets in the Sydney region
  • Compliance: Aligned with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988

Your data never leaves Australian jurisdiction unless you explicitly request it.

Infrastructure Security

Modern, Isolated, Minimised Attack Surface

We utilise modern, ephemeral infrastructure to minimise attack surface and maximise security.

Platform Architecture

  • Compute: Applications run on Fly.io, utilising Firecracker microVMs for strong isolation between tenants
  • Database: Turso (libSQL) or managed PostgreSQL, encrypted at rest
  • Network: Private networking with no direct database exposure to the internet

Encryption Standards

  • In Transit: All data encrypted via TLS 1.2/1.3
  • At Rest: Industry-standard AES-256 encryption
  • Secrets: All credentials and API keys stored in secure vaults, never in code

Application Security

Defense in Depth

Authentication

We use secure, token-based authentication (OIDC/OAuth2) or secure session management. No passwords stored in plain text, ever.

Access Control

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) ensures users only see data relevant to their role. Principle of least privilege applied throughout.

Vulnerability Management

  • Regular dependency scanning for known vulnerabilities
  • Automated security patching of the underlying platform
  • Secure development practices with code review

Privacy Act Compliance

Your Obligations, Supported

HSTLES acts as the data processor. We support your compliance obligations under the Privacy Act 1988.

Our Commitments

  • No data selling: We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • Limited access: We only access your data for support and maintenance purposes
  • Breach notification: Immediate notification in the event of a confirmed data breach (Notifiable Data Breaches scheme)
  • Data portability: You can export your data at any time
  • Data deletion: Upon request, we will delete your data in accordance with our data retention policy

Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)

Our practices are designed to help you comply with the 13 Australian Privacy Principles, including:

  • APP 1 - Open and transparent management of personal information
  • APP 6 - Use or disclosure of personal information
  • APP 8 - Cross-border disclosure of personal information
  • APP 11 - Security of personal information
