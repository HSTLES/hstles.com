The HSTLES Engagement Model

A structured process that gets you from legacy to modern without the traditional development pain

1. Free Initial Consultation

We start with a no-cost, no-obligation session where we:
• Walk through your existing application and workflows
• Understand the data you handle and outcomes you need
• Identify quick wins and risks

If we both agree there's a good fit, we move forward with a structured engagement.

2. Scope Pack - Clarity Before Code

Before anyone spends real money, we produce a concise Scope Pack that includes:
• Problem and outcomes (what we're solving, how success is measured)
• In scope (core workflows, user types, integrations)
• Out of scope (future features not part of this phase)
• Assumptions (data formats, access requirements, dependencies)

This becomes the single source of truth for what we're actually building.

3. Low Initial Engagement Fee

Instead of a large traditional development quote, we charge a one-time low engagement fee that:
• Is intentionally far less than a standard full custom build
• Secures the engagement and commitment from both sides
• Unlocks the full storyboard, design, and build process

When your solution goes live, this fee is converted into credit for your first months of platform access - so you're not paying twice.

4. Co-Design and Build

We don't just take a brief and disappear. We work alongside your team to:
• Storyboard and map key workflows
• Design screens and interactions
• Implement your application inside the HSTLES platform

You see progress, give feedback, and help shape the end product.

5. Clear Completion and Warranty

We break delivery into three stages:
1. Storyboard sign-off - You confirm the proposed flows and screen layouts
2. UAT (User Acceptance Testing) - We deploy to test environment, you run scenarios, we fix defects
3. Production go-live and completion - Once agreed scenarios pass, the project is complete

A warranty period begins where we fix scope-related defects at no extra cost.

6. Fixed-Term Lease with SLAs

Your custom application runs on our infrastructure as a leased service:
• Fixed-term, predictable pricing
• Hosting, maintenance, and monitoring included
• SLAs for uptime, response times, and support
• Regular backups and operational care as part of the service

You get the benefits of a bespoke application without owning and operating the underlying platform.

7. Simple Path for Changes and New Features

Businesses change. So we make enhancements easy:
• You raise a feature request or change
• We review and confirm if it's a defect (in scope) or enhancement (new work)
• Enhancements are priced with bespoke mini quotes and scheduled as small follow-on work

This keeps your application evolving without restarting from zero every time.
