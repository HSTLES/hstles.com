How It Works

The HSTLES engagement model

A structured process from legacy to modern without the traditional development pain.

1. Free Consultation

Walk through your existing application and workflows. Understand the data you handle and outcomes you need. Identify quick wins and risks. No cost, no obligation.

2. Scope Pack

A concise document covering problem and outcomes, in-scope workflows, out-of-scope items, and assumptions. The single source of truth for what we're building.

3. Low Engagement Fee

Intentionally far less than a standard build. Secures commitment from both sides. When your solution goes live, this fee converts to credit for your first months of platform access.

4. Co-Design & Build

We work alongside your team to storyboard key workflows, design screens and interactions, and implement your application. You see progress, give feedback, and shape the end product.

5. Clear Completion

Storyboard sign-off, UAT testing, and production go-live in three clear stages. A warranty period covers scope-related defects at no extra cost.

6. Fixed-Term Lease

Your custom application runs on our infrastructure as a leased service. Fixed-term pricing, hosting, maintenance, monitoring, and SLA-backed support all included.
