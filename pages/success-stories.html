Regional Equipment Hire

A family-owned equipment hire business in regional NSW managing over 200 pieces of equipment across two depots. Their system was a mess: shared Excel spreadsheet on Dropbox (frequently corrupted), wall calendars at each depot (often out of sync), sticky notes for maintenance reminders, and invoices generated manually in Word.

Double-bookings were common. Invoices were forgotten. The owner estimated $2,000-3,000/month lost to missed charges and inefficiency.

  • ✓ Equipment registry with service history, location, availability
  • ✓ Booking calendar with real-time view across both depots
  • ✓ Pre-hire checklists with digital sign-off

Orders came from everywhere: phone calls scribbled on notepads, emails in shared inboxes, walk-in customers, and builder portal orders requiring manual re-entry.

Jobs were tracked on whiteboards, quotes lived in desktop folders, and delivery scheduling was a daily argument about which truck went where.
