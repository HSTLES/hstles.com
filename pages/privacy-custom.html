Custom Solutions Privacy Policy

Your data. Your control. Our secure platform.

Data Ownership & Isolation

You own your data. When we build a custom solution for you, your business data belongs to you, not HSTLES. We act as a data processor on your behalf, not a controller. You control who accesses your data and can export or migrate at any time.

Isolated environments: Each solution runs in its own secure environment with dedicated databases, isolated authentication, and no data sharing between clients. S1/S2 tiers use logical separation; S3 Premium offers dedicated compute and optional private networking.

Minimal HSTLES access: We access your data only during development (with your test data), for support (with permission), platform maintenance, and security updates. All access is logged and auditable.


Multi-Layer Security

Transport: TLS 1.3 for client communications, mTLS for internal services, VL1 mesh networking with Noise Protocol encryption for platform traffic - aligned with ACSC Essential Eight security controls.

Application: Role-based access control tailored to your solution, JWT authentication with scope permissions, API rate limiting, DDoS protection, input validation and injection prevention per Australian Signals Directorate (ASD) guidelines.

Data: AES-256 database encryption at rest, encrypted backups in Australian regions (Sydney available), key rotation and management, optional client-managed keys (S3 tier). Staff access requires support ticket approval, MFA, and all access is time-limited and logged per Privacy Act requirements.


Compliance & Your Rights

Australian Privacy Act 1988: Full compliance with Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), Sydney region hosting available, Notifiable Data Breaches scheme compliance, assistance with Privacy Impact Assessments. Healthcare: Privacy Act healthcare provider obligations, enhanced audit logging, 24-48hr breach notification to OAIC. International: GDPR DPA available, SOC 2/ISO 27001 controls supported (S3 tier).

Data Retention: Active during service agreement, custom policies within your solution, backups per SLA (30-90 days), all compliant with Australian data sovereignty requirements. At end of service: full export + deletion (30 days production, 90 days backups), optional extended read-only retention, or solution handover with source code.

Your Rights (APPs): Access (APP 12), correction (APP 13), complaint to OAIC. Contact: privacy@hstles.com or your account manager. We respond within 30 days per Privacy Act requirements.
