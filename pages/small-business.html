Common questions

What counts as a "device"?

Desktops, laptops, and tablets that need management and support. Servers are priced separately. Phones can be included for an additional $15/month if you need mobile device management.

What if I call too much?

There's no such thing. Call as much as you need. That's the point of fixed pricing. We'd rather you call early about small issues than wait until they become big problems.

Do you support Macs?

Yes. Windows, macOS, and mixed environments. Same price.

How quickly do you respond?

Most calls answered within an hour during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm). Urgent issues (can't work at all) are prioritized immediately.

We're already with another IT company. Can you help us switch?

Absolutely. We handle the transition. We'll coordinate with your current provider to get access details and documentation. You don't need to do anything technical.

What about our internet and phones?

We can help troubleshoot, but we don't manage phone lines or internet connections directly. We can recommend providers if you need them.
