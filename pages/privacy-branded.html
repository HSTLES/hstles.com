Branded Products Privacy Policy

calndr.app, wrkly.app, deposs.app, contrct.app, encntr.app, ORBTR, FRGHT, and CareLedger.

What We Collect

Account Information: Email, display name (optional), OAuth provider details, and subscription/billing data — all encrypted and used solely to provide services.

Usage Data: Authentication events, API metadata, and error logs for security and reliability — we do NOT track detailed behavior or location data.

Service Data: calndr.app stores calendar credentials and sync preferences. wrkly.app stores org structure and roster data. deposs.app stores transaction and wallet metadata. contrct.app stores job listings and contractor profiles. encntr.app stores encounter records and scheduling data. ORBTR stores device configs and metrics. FRGHT stores consignment and logistics data. CareLedger stores healthcare data (Privacy Act compliant, zero-knowledge encrypted).

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

CareLedger uses zero-knowledge encryption: your encryption keys never leave your device. Files are encrypted client-side before upload. Keys are derived from your password and stored locally.

We cannot decrypt your data. Even under legal compulsion, we cannot provide plaintext contents because we don't have the keys. Files are split into encrypted fragments distributed across multiple nodes.

Account Recovery: If you lose your password, we cannot recover your files. Keep backup keys secure. Legal requests only yield encrypted fragments and metadata, not actual contents.

How We Use & Protect Data

Use: Service delivery, authentication, billing, support, and anonymized performance improvements. We NEVER sell data, use it for external advertising, or share with third parties except as required by Australian law or the Privacy Act 1988.

Security: TLS 1.3 for all communications, mTLS for internal services, VL1 mesh with Noise Protocol, database encryption at rest, RBAC at every endpoint, MFA support, regular audits compliant with Australian Cyber Security Centre (ACSC) Essential Eight guidelines.

Healthcare (CareLedger): Privacy Act 1988 compliant with Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), PHI encrypted with audit logs, breach notification within 24-48 hours per Notifiable Data Breaches scheme, logs retained 7 years.

Your Rights: Access, correction, deletion, complaint to OAIC. Contact: privacy@hstles.com
