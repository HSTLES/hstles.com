What to expect from calndr.app, wrkly.app, deposs.app, contrct.app, encntr.app, ORBTR, FRGHT, and CareLedger.

No Formal SLA Offered

Important: Our branded SaaS products are offered without formal Service Level Agreements. We operate on a best-efforts basis.

What This Means: We aim for high availability and work hard to maintain reliable services, but we do not guarantee specific uptime percentages, response times, or service credits for outages.

Need Guaranteed SLAs? Consider our Custom Solutions service where we build your application on the HSTLES platform with formal SLA tiers (S1/S2/S3), guaranteed response times, and uptime commitments.

Status Updates: Real-time service status available at status.hstles.com with incident notifications.

Best-Efforts Support

Community Support: Help documentation, FAQs, and community forums available for all users. We encourage users to help each other.

Email Support: Contact support@hstles.com for technical issues. We aim to respond during Australian business hours (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm AEST/AEDT) but response times are not guaranteed.

Priority: Critical service-wide outages affecting all users receive priority attention. Individual account issues handled as time permits.

No Guaranteed Response Times: Unlike our Custom Solutions SLA tiers, branded products have no contractual response time commitments.

Data Protection & Your Rights

Backups: We perform regular backups as part of good operational practice, but restoration is provided on a best-efforts basis only. Zero-knowledge services (CareLedger) mean we cannot access your encrypted data.

Your Responsibility: For critical data, maintain your own backups. We recommend exporting important data regularly. User-deleted files may not be recoverable.

Australian Consumer Law: Despite no formal SLA, you retain all rights under ACL including consumer guarantees for services. Nothing in our terms limits your statutory rights under Australian law.

Questions: Read our Privacy Policy or contact support@hstles.com
