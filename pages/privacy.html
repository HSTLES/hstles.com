HSTLES Privacy Policy

Protecting your data across our platform and services. Privacy by design, zero-knowledge encryption, and defence in depth.

Core Privacy Principles

HSTLES is built on a foundation of privacy by design. Our distributed mesh architecture, zero-knowledge encryption for storage services, and defense-in-depth security model ensure your data remains protected across all our services.

We collect only what's necessary to provide our services, and we never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

For service-specific privacy details, see our dedicated privacy policies for Branded SaaS products or Custom SaaS services.

Data Protection & Requests

For our branded storage services, we employ distributed file splitting and encryption methodologies that ensure even we cannot access your data. Fragment references are stored only in encrypted user databases.

In response to lawful requests, we can provide limited account information (email, user ID, OAuth methods, billing information) but cannot provide file contents or decrypted data from our zero-knowledge storage services.

We do not log location data or detailed access timestamps beyond what is necessary for security monitoring.

Platform Security

Our infrastructure uses multiple layers of security: VL1 overlay networking with Noise Protocol encryption, mTLS for all internal communications, and role-based access control at every level.

For custom SaaS clients, data isolation and security measures are defined in your specific service agreement and SLA tier.

All HSTLES services undergo regular security audits and follow industry best practices for data protection and incident response.
