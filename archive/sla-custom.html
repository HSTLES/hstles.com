Custom Solutions SLA

Service Level Agreement for HSTLES Platform Solutions

SLA Tiers Overview

Standard (S1): Mon-Fri 9am-5pm AEST/AEDT, 99.5% availability, P1 response within 2 business hours. Email & ticket portal. For non-critical business systems.

Enhanced (S2): Mon-Fri 8am-8pm AEST/AEDT, 99.9% availability, P1 response within 1 hour. Email, portal, messaging. For important business workflows.

Premium (S3): 24×7 for P1 Critical, 99.9% availability, P1 response within 30 minutes. Email, portal, phone for P1. For business-critical systems requiring rapid support.

All times in Australian Eastern Standard/Daylight Time (AEST/AEDT). Public holidays excluded except for P1 Premium tier.


Incident Severity & Response

P1 Critical: Complete outage or critical process blocked. S1: 2hr response, 1 day workaround. S2: 1hr response, 8hr workaround. S3: 30min response 24×7, 4hr workaround.

P2 High: Major degradation with workaround. S1: 4hr response, 3 days resolution. S2: 2hr response, 2 days. S3: 1hr response, 1 day.

P3 Medium: Feature malfunction, non-critical. S1: Next business day, 5 days. S2: 1 day, 4 days. S3: 1 day, 3 days.

P4 Low: Cosmetic, questions, minor issues. S1: 3 days. S2: 2 days. S3: 1-2 days. Best efforts, combined with releases.


Availability, Backups & Credits

Availability: Measured monthly excluding scheduled maintenance (48hr notice) and excluded events (force majeure, third-party failures, client-caused issues).

Backups: Daily production backups, 30+ day retention. RPO/RTO: S1 (24hr/24hr), S2 (12hr/12hr), S3 (4hr/8hr). Restoration for HSTLES-caused data loss.

Service Credits (Optional): 0.5-1.0% below target = 5% credit. 1.0-2.0% below = 10%. >2.0% below = 20% of monthly fees. Credits apply to future platform fees, non-refundable.
