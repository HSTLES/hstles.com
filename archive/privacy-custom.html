Custom Solutions Privacy Policy

Your data. Your control. Our secure platform.

Data Ownership & Isolation

You own your data. When we build a custom solution for you, your business data belongs to you, not HSTLES. We act as a data processor on your behalf, not a controller. You control who accesses your data and can export or migrate at any time.

Isolated environments: Each solution runs in its own secure environment with dedicated databases, isolated authentication, and no data sharing between clients. S1/S2 tiers use logical separation; S3 Premium offers dedicated compute and optional private networking.

Minimal HSTLES access: We access your data only during development (with your test data), for support (with permission), platform maintenance, and security updates. All access is logged and auditable.


Multi-Layer Security

Transport: TLS 1.3 for client communications, mTLS for internal services, VL1 mesh networking with Noise Protocol encryption for platform traffic - aligned with ACSC Essential Eight security controls.

Application: Role-based access control tailored to your solution, JWT authentication with scope permissions, API rate limiting, DDoS protection, input validation and injection prevention per Australian Signals Directorate (ASD) guidelines.

Data: AES-256 database encryption at rest, encrypted backups in Australian regions (Sydney available), key rotation and management, optional client-managed keys (S3 tier). Staff access requires support ticket approval, MFA, and all access is time-limited and logged per Privacy Act requirements.


Compliance & Your Rights

Australian Privacy Act 1988: Full compliance with Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), Sydney region hosting available, Notifiable Data Breaches scheme compliance, assistance with Privacy Impact Assessments. Healthcare: Privacy Act healthcare provider obligations, enhanced audit logging, 24-48hr breach notification to OAIC. International: GDPR DPA available, SOC 2/ISO 27001 controls supported (S3 tier).

Data Retention: Active during service agreement, custom policies within your solution, backups per SLA (30-90 days), all compliant with Australian data sovereignty requirements. At end of service: full export + deletion (30 days production, 90 days backups), optional extended read-only retention, or solution handover with source code.

Your Rights (APPs): Access (APP 12), correction (APP 13), complaint to OAIC. Contact: privacy@hstles.com or your account manager. We respond within 30 days per Privacy Act requirements.

Custom SaaS Privacy Commitment

When we modernize your application onto the HSTLES platform, your data remains yours.
This policy explains how we handle data during and after custom solution development.

Core Privacy Principles

1. Your Data Ownership

You own your data. When we build a custom solution for your organization:
• Your business data belongs to you, not to HSTLES
• You control who has access to your data
• We act as a data processor on your behalf, not a data controller
• You can export or migrate your data at any time

2. Data Isolation

Your solution is isolated. Each custom solution runs in its own secure environment:
• Dedicated database instances with encryption at rest
• Isolated authentication and identity management
• No data sharing between clients unless explicitly configured
• Logical and/or physical separation based on your SLA tier

3. Minimal HSTLES Access

We access your data only when necessary:
• During initial development and testing (with your test data)
• For technical support when you grant permission
• For platform maintenance and security updates
• To investigate reported issues or performance problems

All access is logged, auditable, and bound by strict confidentiality obligations.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We never sell or share your data.
• Your business data is not used for HSTLES marketing or product development
• We don't share data with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent
• Subprocessors (hosting, infrastructure) are carefully vetted and bound by confidentiality agreements

Information We Collect

During the Engagement

Discovery Phase:
• Business requirements and workflow descriptions
• Sample data and schemas for development (you control what you provide)
• User role definitions and access requirements
• Integration endpoints and API specifications

Development Phase:
• Test data you provide for UAT environments
• Feedback and change requests during storyboarding
• Bug reports and testing outcomes

During Production Operation

Platform & Application Data:
• Your business data as defined in the Scope Pack
• User authentication and identity information
• Application logs (errors, warnings, access events)
• Performance metrics (response times, resource usage)

HSTLES Access Logs:
• Support access timestamps and actions
• Deployment and maintenance activities
• Security monitoring events

We Do NOT Collect

• Data beyond what's necessary for your solution's functionality
• Personal data about your end users for HSTLES purposes
• Aggregated analytics for HSTLES product improvement (unless agreed separately)
• Marketing or behavioral data for non-contracted purposes

How We Use Your Data

HSTLES uses data solely to provide, maintain, and improve your custom solution:

Service Delivery

• Running your application on our platform infrastructure
• Processing requests from your end users
• Storing and retrieving data as required by your solution's logic
• Providing authentication and authorization services

Support & Maintenance

• Diagnosing and resolving technical issues you report
• Investigating performance degradation or errors
• Applying security patches and platform updates
• Conducting routine maintenance during agreed windows

Security & Monitoring

• Detecting and responding to security threats
• Monitoring uptime and availability per your SLA
• Logging access and changes for audit purposes
• Backing up data per your disaster recovery requirements

Legal & Compliance

• Meeting our contractual obligations to you
• Complying with applicable laws and regulations
• Responding to valid legal processes (with notice to you where permitted)
• Enforcing our terms and protecting our rights

Data Isolation & Security

Isolated Environments

Each custom solution operates in a logically isolated environment:

Standard (S1) & Enhanced (S2) Tiers:
• Dedicated database schemas with row-level security
• Application-level isolation with strict RBAC
• Shared infrastructure with logical separation

Premium (S3) Tier:
• Dedicated database instances
• Isolated compute resources (dedicated VMs or containers)
• Optional private networking and VPN access
• Enhanced audit logging and compliance controls

Multi-Layer Security

Transport Layer:
• TLS 1.3 for all client communications
• mTLS for internal service-to-service calls
• VL1 encrypted mesh networking for platform traffic

Application Layer:
• Role-based access control tailored to your solution
• JWT authentication with scope-based permissions
• API rate limiting and DDoS protection
• Input validation and SQL injection prevention

Data Layer:
• Database encryption at rest (AES-256)
• Encrypted backups stored in separate regions
• Key management with rotation and separation
• Optional client-managed encryption keys (Premium tier)

Access Controls

HSTLES staff access to your solution is restricted:
• No direct database access without support ticket and approval
• All access is logged with timestamp, user, and actions
• Privileged access requires multi-factor authentication
• Access is granted on a need-to-know basis and time-limited

Data Retention & Deletion

During Active Service

• Your application data is retained as long as your service agreement is active
• You control data retention policies within your solution (e.g., archiving old records)
• Backups are retained per your SLA (typically 30-90 days rolling)
• Audit logs are retained per your compliance requirements (often 7 years for regulated industries)

End of Service

When your service agreement ends:

Option 1: Data Export & Deletion (Standard)
• You receive a full data export in agreed formats (SQL, JSON, CSV, etc.)
• After confirmation of successful export, all production data is deleted within 30 days
• Backups are permanently deleted within 90 days

Option 2: Extended Retention (By Agreement)
• Data can be retained in read-only mode for an agreed period (e.g., 6-12 months)
• Useful for transition periods or regulatory hold requirements
• Incurs reduced monthly fees for storage and access

Option 3: Solution Handover
• We provide complete source code, configurations, and deployment documentation
• You migrate the solution to your own infrastructure
• HSTLES assists with migration and knowledge transfer

Billing & Contract Records

Financial records are retained separately for:
• 7 years for tax compliance (Australia/US requirements)
• Contract documents and Scope Packs retained for 7 years post-completion
• These do not include your business data, only engagement metadata

Third-Party Services (Subprocessors)

HSTLES uses carefully selected third-party services to operate the platform. These are bound by Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) and confidentiality obligations:

Infrastructure Providers

Fly.io - Primary hosting infrastructure
• Location: Multiple regions (US, EU, Asia-Pacific)
• Purpose: Compute, networking, and edge deployment
• Data processed: Application code, user requests, encrypted data

Turso (libSQL) - Database hosting
• Location: Configurable per client (default: US/EU)
• Purpose: Database storage and replication
• Data processed: Encrypted database contents, metadata

Both providers offer DPAs and comply with GDPR, SOC 2, and industry security standards.

Payment Processing

• Stripe or similar for subscription billing (for lease payments)
• We do not store full credit card details
• PCI DSS compliant payment handling

Communication & Support

• Email service providers for notifications and support
• Support ticketing systems (hosted securely)
• Video conferencing for remote support (by agreement, with client consent)

Client-Specific Integrations

Your custom solution may integrate with third-party services you specify:
• Payment gateways, shipping APIs, CRM systems, etc.
• You are responsible for agreeing to third-party terms
• We implement integrations securely (encrypted credentials, minimal scope)
• Third-party data handling is documented in your Scope Pack

Your Privacy Rights

Access & Transparency

You can request:
• Full data export from your solution at any time
• Access logs showing HSTLES staff access to your environment
• Details of any security incidents affecting your data
• Information about subprocessors and data transfer locations

Control & Portability

You have the right to:
• Export all data in machine-readable formats
• Migrate your solution to another provider (with assistance)
• Request changes to data processing or access controls
• Terminate the service and have data deleted per retention schedule

End User Rights (Your Customers)

If your solution processes personal data of your end users (customers, patients, employees), you are the data controller and responsible for:
• Informing your users about data processing in your own privacy policy
• Handling user access, correction, and deletion requests
• Obtaining necessary consents for data processing

HSTLES will assist you in meeting these obligations by providing:
• Data export tools built into your solution
• API endpoints for programmatic data access
• Audit logs for compliance reporting
• DPA and subprocessor documentation

How to Exercise Your Rights

Email: privacy@hstles.com
Support Portal: https://login.hstles.com/support

Compliance & Regulations

GDPR (EU Customers)

For EU-based clients or solutions processing EU user data:
• HSTLES acts as a data processor under GDPR
• We sign Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with Standard Contractual Clauses
• EU data can be hosted in EU regions (Fly.io Frankfurt, Amsterdam)
• Data transfers outside EU comply with adequacy decisions or SCCs
• We assist with DPIA (Data Protection Impact Assessment) if required

Healthcare Data (Privacy Act 1988)

For solutions handling healthcare information in Australia:
• We comply with Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
• Enhanced audit logging and access controls
• Encryption at rest and in transit meeting healthcare standards
• Breach notification procedures (within 24-48 hours, per NDB scheme)
• Data handling agreements cover platform infrastructure and specified subprocessors

SOC 2 / ISO 27001

HSTLES platform is designed to support SOC 2 and ISO 27001 controls:
• Regular security audits and penetration testing
• Documented security policies and incident response procedures
• Vendor management and subprocessor due diligence
• Continuous monitoring and alerting

Premium (S3) tier clients can request audit reports and compliance documentation.

Industry-Specific Regulations

We can support solutions in regulated industries:
• Financial services (PCI DSS, banking regulations)
• Education (FERPA, COPPA)
• Government (FedRAMP, FISMA considerations)

Compliance requirements are documented in your Scope Pack and Order Form.

Security Incidents & Breach Notification

Incident Response

If we detect a security incident affecting your solution:
1. Immediate containment - Stop the incident from spreading
2. Notification - Inform you within 24-48 hours (faster for P1 Critical)
3. Investigation - Determine root cause and impact scope
4. Remediation - Fix vulnerabilities and restore service
5. Post-incident report - Detailed timeline and corrective actions

Data Breach Notification

For breaches involving personal data:
• GDPR requires notification within 72 hours - we notify you immediately so you can meet this deadline
• Privacy Act 1988 requires notification per Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme - we notify within 24 hours
• We provide details needed for your breach notifications to regulators and affected individuals
• We assist with breach investigation and remediation

Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:
• Notifying regulators and affected individuals per applicable laws
• Determining whether a security incident constitutes a reportable breach
• Documenting breach notifications for compliance audits

We provide the technical details and support; you handle legal/regulatory obligations as the data controller.

International Data Transfers

HSTLES operates infrastructure in multiple regions:

Data Residency

You can specify where your solution's data is hosted:
• United States: Default for US-based clients (Fly.io US regions)

Cross-Border Transfers

Transfer mechanisms are documented in your DPA or data handling agreement.

Platform Services

HSTLES platform services (authentication, monitoring) may operate in multiple regions:
• Metadata and logs may be centralized for operational efficiency
• Sensitive business data remains in your specified region
• Premium tier can request region-locked operations
