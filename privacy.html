Our Privacy Policy

Requests for Information

Due to the method in which we distribute the data & lack of built-in logging, we are unable to specify the location of the data, combining that with the fragment references only being stored in the users encrypted SQLite database, ensures a users data is secure from us or anybody attempting to make us provide user data.

The most we are able to provide to authorities is an export of a users entry within our database, being a users email, userid, oAuth methods, billing information & salted internal references.

Additionally we do not store login data such as location or time information.

Our Storage Engine

We are unable to provide details or elaboration on our storage engine without revealing specific enough information on the splitting encryption & edge distribution methodologies, due to the potential of a competitor being able to replicate a similar enough solution.
